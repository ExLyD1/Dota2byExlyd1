Dota 2

Aboute Game

Dota 2, Defense of the Ancients is a multiplayer video game in the MOBA subgenre, a standalone extension of the DotA card ideas for Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos and its modifications Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne. Dota 2 was released by Valve Corporation for the Microsoft Windows platform in a public beta in 2011. The game was released for OS X and Linux on July 18, 2013. Initially, Dota 2 used the Source gaming engine, and in 2015 it was ported to Source 2. Dota 2 was the first game to use this gamіng engine. Dota 2 is distributed and supported exclusively by Steam.

All Heroes

Strength Heroes

These heroes differ from the others by their great ability to resist damage, they are mostly used as initiators and offlaners.

Agility Heroes

Agility heroes are usually selected to fulfill the role of carry, although there are also exceptions, such as Bounty Hunter who can play in the role of support.

Intelligence Heroes

These heroes have many support skills, which is why they are considered the best supports. Although there are also exceptions such as Storm Spirit and Invoker, who can be played mid as cores.
